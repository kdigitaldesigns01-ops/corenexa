From Diagnosis to Execution

Most finance inefficiencies are not caused by one isolated issue. They usually sit across configuration, process design, reporting, reconciliations, and user workarounds.

That is why we start with diagnosis.

Our services are designed to identify what is slowing your finance function down, prioritise what needs to change, and support practical improvement.

SAP Finance Optimisation

We assess how your SAP finance environment is configured, used, and aligned to business requirements.

Typical focus areas:

  • SAP FICO configuration review
  • General Ledger, AP, AR, Asset Accounting and CO process alignment
  • Finance process bottlenecks
  • Underused SAP functionality
  • Post-go-live optimisation opportunities

A clearer, more efficient SAP finance environment that better supports reporting, control, and decision-making.

Month-End Close Improvement

We identify what is slowing down your close process and where manual intervention is creating delays or risk.

Typical focus areas:

  • Close process mapping
  • Manual journal and reconciliation review
  • Dependency and approval bottlenecks
  • Data quality issues
  • Close calendar optimisation

A faster, more controlled close process with fewer manual workarounds.

Reconciliation & Manual Workaround Reduction

We help finance teams identify where manual reconciliations, spreadsheets, and repeated interventions are compensating for SAP process or configuration gaps.

Typical focus areas:

  • Manual reconciliation mapping
  • Root-cause analysis
  • Process redesign opportunities
  • Automation candidates
  • Control and audit risk reduction

Reduced manual effort, improved accuracy, and stronger finance control.

Reporting & Financial Visibility

Typical focus areas:

  • Reporting structure review
  • Data flow and source-of-truth assessment
  • Margin, cost, and profitability visibility
  • Management reporting gaps
  • Real-time visibility opportunities

Better insight into financial performance, cost drivers, and operational decision-making.
